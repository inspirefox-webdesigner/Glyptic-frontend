Welcome to Glyptic

Glyptic means “An art of engraving precious stone”

We are a global industrial technology leader bringing world-leading expertise in Fire Detection & Alarm, Fire Protection, Fire Suppression and Automation to smart industries, resilient infrastructure, future-proof data centers and intelligent buildings. Anchored by our deep domain expertise, we provide integrated end-to-end lifecycle Fire Safety solutions with connected modern innovative products, automation, and services, delivering classic ‘state of the Art’ Solution to enable profitable growth for our customers.

We are a people company with an ecosystem of Hundred plus colleagues and numerous Business partners operating in India to ensure proximity to our customers. We embrace diversity and inclusion in everything we do, guided by our meaningful purpose of a sustainable future for all.

GLYPTIC is one Amongst very few Technology-leader in India has the capacity to provide service support for end-to-end solution of multiple Fire Detection & Alarm brands being use in the industry. Customer gets relief to have a comprehensive unified solutions and services of multi-brand proprietary Fire Detection products under a single contractor as most of the industries has dissimilar pre-installed Fire Detection system at various plants. Customer face challenges of technological diversity and OEM monopoly..

Aside Conventional Fire detection & Alarm we have GLYPTIC own Brand modern and latest technological inventive Early and affluent Fire detection products such as Flame & Smoke Detection Camera, Distributed Temperature Sensing FO-LHS controller integrated to Fire Alarm System including Digital LHS Cable. Instant Self-Detection and Protection System FIPRON CORD, BLAZRCUT & AEROSOL system for Electrical Panel.

Leader in Fire Protection (Hydrant & Sprinkler, HVWS, MVWS), Room Flooding Gas Suppression System and Chemical Tank Foam Protection System.

End-to-End Solution begins from Fire consultancy, Fire Audit calculation Fire Load, Preparation of Optimizing BOQ, Project Management and Post DLP Service support maintaining extensive Spares Stock at 10000+ Square-Feet Warehouse.
