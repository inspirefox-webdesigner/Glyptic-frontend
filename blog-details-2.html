Glyptic Annual General Meeting 2025 – A Year of Growth, Vision & Innovation

We are thrilled to announce the successful completion of Glyptic’s Annual General Meeting (AGM) 2025, held on 9th April, 2025 at Regenta Orko'ss Hotel, marking another milestone in our journey of excellence in fire detection, protection, and automation systems. This year’s AGM brought together our dedicated team members, partners, and stakeholders to reflect on our achievements, review financial and strategic outcomes, and align our goals for the year ahead.

🔹 Key Highlights from AGM 2025: -Year-in-Review Presentation showcasing major project completions across India and abroad. -Product Innovation Discussion, focusing on future-ready fire alarm solutions and system integrations. -Training & Development Focus, unveiling our upcoming Fire Alarm Training Program to empower the next generation of engineers and technicians. -Vision 2026: Launching new initiatives in smart safety technologies, expanded service coverage, and global partnerships.

Our Managing Directors shared an inspiring keynote, emphasizing Glyptic’s commitment to innovation, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement. The meeting concluded with an open Q&A session, team recognition awards, taking an oath and a renewed energy to drive Glyptic forward. Stay tuned for more updates and opportunities as we step into an exciting new chapter of growth and transformation.
